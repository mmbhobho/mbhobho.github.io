    • PROBLEM STATEMENT

    The system used by taxi drivers to find passenders is inefficient.

    1. Petrol is wasted as taxi drivers blindly search for passengers.
    2. Driving in search of passengers is tiring and can be fraustrating for taxi driver.
    3. Passengers spend too much time waiting for a taxi. Osizweni to Madadeni taxis are the worst.
    4. Amaphara charge R2.00 when they refer a passenger to a taxi.

    PROPOSED SOLUTION

    Peer to peer phone application for taxi drivers and passengers.
    Unlike Uber, the system does not seek out to get rid of the current taxi transport system. It is seeking to help both drivers and passengers.

    The proposed solution is to create a platform where passengers and taxi drivers can share their locations.

    1. Develop a lite Android Application for both taxi drivers and passengers.
    2. An integrated Web Application for other operating systems.
